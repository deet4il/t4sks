App Guide

The Purpose: This app turns your to-do lists into a focused adventure.

Gym Battles: Every 3 levels, engage in a 3v3 battle. Win to earn Badges and permanent buffs.

Siege Style: Boss Pokémon retain their HP if you withdraw or lose. Keep chipping away!

Unlimited Energy: There is no cap on BE. Stash energy for major battles.

Roster Snapshot: You may only use Pokémon caught at or before the gym's required level.

Master Ball: Earn charges by finishing tasks. Use them to catch legendary Pokémon for your milestones.
