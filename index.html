App Guide

The Purpose

This app turns your to-do lists into a manageable "Active Campaign." It’s designed to stop you from feeling scattered by turning your daily grind into a focused adventure.

Core Mechanics

  • Catching Pokémon: Every time you add a task, a wild Pokémon appears to "guard" it. When you finish that task, you catch the Pokémon and add it to your personal Pokedex.
  • Shiny Luck: Sometimes you’ll get lucky! There is a rare chance to find a "Shiny" Pokémon with a special color and a sparkle in your collection. These appear randomly as a reward for your hard work and consistency.
  • Mission Arcs: Organize your life into different "Arcs" (like Work, Home, or Hobbies). This lets you focus on one area at a time so your brain doesn't feel cluttered.
  • Master Ball Economy: Think of this as your priority engine. You earn a Master Ball charge by finishing 5 smaller "maintenance" tasks. Use that charge to power up and tackle your biggest, most important goal for the day.
  • Accordion Tasks: Don't let long notes ruin your view. Tap any task to "unroll" it and see all your detailed thoughts, then tap it again to hide them and keep your list looking sleek.
  • Leveling & XP: Every task you finish earns you XP. Watch your level grow and unlock new Trainer Ranks as you stay consistent and build momentum over time.
